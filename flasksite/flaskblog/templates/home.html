INTRODUCTION


Banana is one of the major food cum fruit crops that have capability to provide abundant income to the farmers and country’s economy. But this capability of banana crop is not fully enjoyed by the farmers as there are various threats and decrease the production.In agriculture sector, computer vision and machine vision systems have been developed for various applications like grading of fruits, vegetables, sorting of fruits, vegetables and detection of weeds.

The technique which we are using to predict and classify the banana disease is Bayesian Networks.Bayesian network is one of the most effective theoretical models for uncertainty knowledge expression and reasoning. It has not only a solid basis for probability theory, but also a perfect correspondence with technical knowledge structures. So we use Bayesian network to model the plant diseases.
